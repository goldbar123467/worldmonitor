▶ Your Mission

Imagine someone hands you a car, a map, and $2,000. Your job is to design the best 7 to 14 day summer road trip across the United States.

You start in Indiana. You end in Indiana. Everything in between is up to you — the states you cross, the cities you sleep in, the parks you hike, and the beaches you find.

This isn't a real trip. You're the planner. Make a route someone else would love to take.

▶ The Rules of the Road

1

Pick Your Length

Plan a trip between 7 and 14 days long. Longer trips = more to plan, but more places to see.

2

Cross 5+ States

Drive through at least five different states. No flying, no teleporting — your crew rides by car.

3

Hit 3 Regions

Visit at least three of the four U.S. regions: Northeast, South, Midwest, or West.

4

One National Park

Make a stop at at least one National Park. Tell us one cool fact about it.

5

One Big City

Spend at least one night in a major U.S. city. Plan a full day of stuff to do there.

6

One Historic Site

Stop at a place where history happened. Be ready to explain why it matters.

7

One Beach Day

Find an ocean, gulf, or Great Lake. Take one full lazy beach day.

▶ What's Inside Your Slideshow

1. Route Map 1 Slide

A U.S. map showing your full route. Label every overnight stop with the city and state. Use a Canva map element or generate one with AI.

2. Day-by-Day Itinerary 3+ Slides

Show every day of the trip. Date, where you sleep, miles driven, and what you'll do. Use a table or a slide per major stop.

3. Budget Tracker 1 Slide

What you spent on gas, food, lodging, and fun. Show daily totals and a running total. Stay under $2,000.

4. Postcard Home 1 Slide

Pick your favorite stop. Write a one-paragraph postcard back to Indiana. Pair it with an AI-generated postcard image of that place.

5. Crew Roster 1 Slide

Up to four people on the trip — real, family, friends, or characters. One sentence each on why they're with you.

▶ Bring It to Life in Canva

Your final project is a Canva slideshow. You'll use AI to generate images, pick from beautiful travel templates, and design like a real graphic designer.

SLIDE 1

The Cover

Trip name, your name, and an AI-generated hero image — like a vintage road trip poster or a sunset highway.

SLIDE 2

Meet the Crew

Up to 4 travel buddies. Use AI to generate cartoon portraits or pick stickers. One sentence per person.

SLIDE 3

The Route

Your map of the U.S. with every stop labeled. Use Canva's map elements or generate a stylized travel map.

SLIDES 4–8

Day-by-Day Stops

One slide per major stop or one big itinerary table. Each major stop gets an AI image of the place.

SLIDE 9

The Budget

A clear table or chart showing how you spent your $2,000. Daily costs and total. Color-code it.

SLIDE 10

The Postcard

Your favorite stop, written like a postcard home, with an AI-generated retro postcard image to match.

★ Your AI Toolkit ★

Magic Media Generate images of places, food, and people from a text prompt.
Magic Write Brainstorm stop ideas, write postcard text, fix grammar.
Canva Templates Search "travel" or "road trip" — start there. Don't begin blank.
Label Your AI Every AI image gets a tiny "AI-generated" tag in the corner.

How to Write Better AI Prompts

"Yellowstone"
"Vintage travel poster of Yellowstone with elk and a geyser at sunset, retro 1970s style"
"Beach"
"Sunny postcard of a Florida beach with palm trees and seagulls, bright watercolor look"
"My crew"
"Cartoon group portrait of four kids on a road trip, smiling, in front of a red minivan"
